BlockChain

The blockchain is a growing series of unchanged record of data.
It is not owned by a single entity and is managed by a group of computers.
It is an easy way of passing information from A to B in an automated and protected manner.
"The blockchain is an incorruptible digital ledger of economic transactions that can be
programmed to record not just financial transactions, but virtually everything of value."
-Don and Alex Trapscott, authors Blockchain Revolution (2016)

Autonomous Things (AuT)

This is a term used to describe the technological developments that will allow computers
to be brought into the physical environment as autonomous entities without human interference.
It will have the ability to move freely and interact with humans and other objects.
Examples:

  • Self-Navagating Drones
  • Autonomous Cars
  • Military Robots

Immersive Experience

This is an illusory environment that commands the senses and completely surrounds you.
It gives you the illusion that you are inside of it and thus a part of it. It makes use of
sound and/or other stimuli that provides an engrossing total environment.
Examples:

  • Virtual Reality
  • Mixed Reality
